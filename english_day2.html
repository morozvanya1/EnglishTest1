Грамматика
Present Simple
Самое используемое время English — 60% всех текстов и речи

📌 Когда использовать Present Simple?

1. Факты и истины: Water boils at 100°C. The sun rises in the east.

2. Привычки и рутина: I drink coffee every morning. She goes to the gym on Mondays.

3. Постоянные состояния: He lives in Moscow. They work at a hospital.

4. Расписания: The train leaves at 9 AM.

Маркеры времени: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never, every day/week/year

✅ Утвердительное предложение

Subject + Verb (base / +s) + Object / Adverb = I work every day. / She works at a school.
МестоимениеГлагол WORKПример
IworkI work at home.
YouworkYou work hard.
He / She / ItworksShe works at a bank.
WeworkWe work together.
TheyworkThey work at night.

⚠️ Правила добавления -s / -es для He/She/It

Обычно +s: work→works, read→reads, play→plays, live→lives

+es (после -s, -sh, -ch, -x, -o): go→goes, watch→watches, teach→teaches, fix→fixes

y→ies (согл.+y): study→studies, fly→flies, carry→carries

Исключения: have→has, be→is, do→does

❌ Отрицательное предложение

Subject + do not / does not + Verb (base)
МестоимениеОтрицаниеКраткая формаПример
I / You / We / Theydo notdon'tI don't like coffee.
He / She / Itdoes notdoesn'tShe doesn't work here.

❓ Вопрос

Do / Does + Subject + Verb (base) + ...?
ВспомогательныйМестоимениеПримерОтвет
DoI / You / We / TheyDo you speak English?Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
DoesHe / She / ItDoes she work here?Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.
💡
Ключевое правило: В вопросах и отрицаниях глагол всегда в базовой форме — никакого -s!
❌ Does she works? → ✅ Does she work?
❌ She doesn't works. → ✅ She doesn't work.

📎 Притяжательные местоимения с семьёй

my — мой/моя  |  your — твой  |  his — его  |  her — её  |  our — наш  |  their — их

My sister is a teacher. His brother works at a hospital. Their parents live in Moscow.

В отличие от русского, притяжательные местоимения не изменяются по роду: «его» всегда his, «её» всегда her.

A Working Day

My name is Victor. I am a programmer. I live in Moscow with my family.

Every morning I wake up at seven o'clock. I drink coffee and read the news. I don't eat much in the morning — just bread and coffee.

I work from home. I write code for eight hours every day. I love my job! It is interesting and pays well.

My wife is a teacher. She teaches mathematics at a school. She leaves home at eight in the morning. She comes back at five in the afternoon.

We have two children — a son and a daughter. Our son studies at university. He wants to be an engineer. Our daughter is only five — she doesn't go to school yet. She plays and draws all day.

In the evening we eat together. We talk, laugh, and rest. My family is the most important thing in my life.
